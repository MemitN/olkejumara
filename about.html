Our Story

Born and raised in the heart of Maasai Mara, Olkeju Mara Tours was founded by local Maasai guides who wanted to share the authentic beauty of their homeland with the world.

What started as a small family-run operation in 2009 has grown into one of Kenya's most trusted safari companies. We are proud to be 100% locally owned and operated, with deep roots in the Maasai community and an intimate knowledge of every corner of the Mara ecosystem.

Our team consists of experienced Maasai guides, conservationists, and hospitality professionals who are passionate about wildlife conservation and cultural preservation. Every safari with us supports local communities and conservation efforts.

Our Mission

To provide authentic, sustainable safari experiences that showcase the incredible wildlife and rich Maasai culture of our homeland, while supporting conservation and community development.

Our Vision

To be the leading locally-owned safari company in Kenya, recognized for our authentic experiences, conservation leadership, and positive impact on Maasai communities.
